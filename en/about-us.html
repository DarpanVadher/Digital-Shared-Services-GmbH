Who we are

Digital Shared Service GmbH is a brand-first, full-service digital agency based in Dresden, Germany, Specializing in digital marketing strategy, creative design, content development, and digital technology solutions.. Founded with a focus on delivering results-driven solutions for brands, Digital Shared Service GmbH brings together a team of experts with diverse strengths to create a powerful, unified force.

We believe in true collaboration—working closely with our clients to understand their unique needs and goals. Our experienced marketing team takes care of all the complex tasks, ensuring smooth and effective execution at every stage. Every project we undertake begins with in-depth brand research, and all our efforts are designed to complement and strengthen the brand identity. With a constant drive for innovation and a passion for creative storytelling, we are always looking ahead, exploring new ideas and pushing boundaries.

Expertise

Your brand is your company’s most valuable asset – let our team of experts support you with a tailored strategy. Our experienced team works within clear, efficient processes to help you overcome internal challenges. We turn insights into impactful strategies and create consistent, meaningful brand experiences.
